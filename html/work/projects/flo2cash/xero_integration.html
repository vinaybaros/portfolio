Send invoice with a
PAY NOW button

Pre-build integration with Xero enable businesses to receive payments directly from electronic payment notices generated out of Xero, and automatically reconcile these payments when received.

payment
gateway xero
integration

Flo2Cash payment gateway can be easily integrated with Xero cloud accounting software. The application enables you to send a Xero invoice with a Pay Now button to your customer. When he clicks on the button, he is directed to a Flo2Cash hosted payment page, where he can fill the credit card/ direct debit details and submit the payment. On successfully processing the payment, Flo2Cash sends a receipt to your customer and marks the invoice as paid via the Xero API. All funds are settled into your account the next day.
