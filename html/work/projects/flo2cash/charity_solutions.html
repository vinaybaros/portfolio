Benefits for charities

Flo2Cash has been accelerating the cashflow of businesses and nonprofits since 2003! With thousands of satisfied clients, you can trust us with credit card and direct debit recurring payment solutions that will fit just right for your needs.

  • Tools to acquire a donor

    Acquiring regular donors is vital for charity organisations. Flo2Cash cloud forms allows you to launch a donation page with in day along with receipts, thankyou emails. We have pre-integrated solution with number of face to face and out bound call centres to setup payments seamlessly.

  • Keep the funds flowing

    Maintaining regular donations flow is vital for charity organisations. Flo2Cash improves your collections by automatically processing incoming donations via credit card and Direct Debit. We retry a missed payment multiple times to increase chances of successful collection.

  • Stay focused

    Flo2Cash’s fully automated and fully managed payment solutions save your organisation time and money, as there’s no more manual processing and reconciling of payments. More importantly, there’s less time needed on admin tasks. Your resources can be devoted to help your cause.
