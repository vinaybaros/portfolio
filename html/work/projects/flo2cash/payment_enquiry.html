Flo2Cash is a payment processing company, which receives your signed direct debit authority, and transfers the money between your Bank account and the Merchant (Retailer/Service Provider/ Charity). We process payments on behalf of these organisations. You might have done a transaction with a company that uses our payment system.

  • Please note that for authentication purposes, Flo2Cash sometimes perform a $1.00 NZD authorization transaction on a credit card.
  • The $1.00 will be released in five to ten days after authorization takes place.
