How it works

QuickInspect is simple and easy to use. All you need to do is download the App, sign-up and start creating custom checklists or choose from available templates. Also, you need to ask your team to download the app and sign-up so that you can share the ready checklists with them.

  • create

    Create customizable or use readymade Inspection checklist

  • send

    Send the checklist electronically to your field staff

  • receive

    The Field Staff quickly fill in the checklist and send them back

  • take action

    Direct the maintenance or service staff to take appropriate action immediately

Mobile Apps

With QuickInspect App you can schedule and assign inspections for your field staff or conduct inspections yourself. The App allows you to fully customize inspection templates or use pre-loaded ones. Moreover, you can upload photos and incorporate them into your inspection reports.
