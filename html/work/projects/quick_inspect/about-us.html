About Us

QuickInspect is built to change the way professional inspections are done. The smart application not only brings efficiency to the inspection process but also minimizes paper usage, making it a green versatile tool for all kinds of inspections, ranging from hotel rooms inspections to inspections of industrial machines.

QuickInspect was born out of a need for a customizable template, for inspecting properties, that could be easily filled and shared. Conventional methods of making inspection reports and sending them to concerned personnel were slow and inefficient. Somebody had to manually pick up inspection checklist from the office, fill them up and drop them back. This took time and further delayed processes dependent on it.

Our team at the time was working on another project that needed inspection reports and somebody asked, “don’t we have an app for that, yet?’ And a month later we had a brand new app, developed by our own team, for carrying out inspections right from our mobiles and tablets. Thus was born Quick Inspect!
