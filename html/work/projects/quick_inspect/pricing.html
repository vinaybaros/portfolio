Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Change a paid plan after using it for some time?

Yes. You may upgrade, downgrade or cancel your current plan any time from the app on your own.

What happens when the trial period ends after 14 days? Will I loose access to the features and not be able to create a template or report?

Once your trial expires, you will not be able to edit or generate new templates or reports. However, your data will be safe with us so that you can resume editing after subscribing to one of our paid packages.

When my trial period ends, would there be a pop-up asking for a payment?

Yes. After the trial period, when you try to generate a report, there will be a message or a pop up on your device saying “Your trial period has expired”.

How many inspections can I carry out as an individual or an organization during my 14 day trial period?

You can execute unlimited inspections during your trial period though you can not share the report externally via email.

I registered as an Individual User and I liked the app so much that I want my organization to use it. Do I need to register with a different email address as an organization and then proceed or would there be an option to upgrade my account from individual to organisation?

If you are the director or owner of the organization, then after 14 day trial period, you can register with us as an organization with an option to choose from multiple plans that we offer.
On the other hand, if you are employee, working for an organization, then you can keep using your account for personal inspections and ask your organization owner/director to create a second account to assign inspections.

If I am not satisfied with the application after paying for it (after the trial period), can I get a refund ? Especially for business users who are paying for the package monthly.

We’d hate to let you go. But, yes you will get a refund on pro-rata basis in all cases. So whether you are an individual or a business, you can cancel your account at any time and request for a refund.

I am inviting a User in my organisation and he is already registered as an Individual. Can he register from the same email id?

As an organisation if you invite a registered user with the same email address that the User has used to register as an individual then there will be an error – the email address is already in use.
Email address has to be unique everytime someone wants to register with us.

Can I register as an individual and an organization using one email address?

No, you can’t. You will need two separate email ids to register as an individual and as an organization
